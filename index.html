Since 1997, industrial gas turbines are what we do.

For over 20 years, we have supported the GG4/FT4 engine and its users with an extensive inventory, fast and reliable shipping, and knowledgeable customer service. To continue our support, we expanded our capabilities into component repair, with our new component repair shop in Southwick, MA as well as field service in a partnership with IGTS.

  • Inventory for every requirement

    • With our extensive inventory of over 10,000 line items we can support the needs of any requirement. From nose cone to exhaust duct, we have you covered.

  • Fast and reliable shipping

    • Orders received before 3:00PM EST can ship same day. Our customers in the northeast can expect next day delivery at UPS Ground prices thanks to our prime location in northern NJ.

  • Experienced customer service

    • Put our years of experience to work for you. We can help identify alternatives to rare or obsolete items, or reverse engineer items per your specifications.

  • What we offer

    • Most items done in our repair shop are kept in stock as rotables for customers with urgent requirements. This includes starters, manifolds, fuel pumps, valves, and much more.

  • How it works

    • Instead of sending your components in for inspection and repair with a lead time, we supply you with an already overhauled unit at a fixed cost that can ship same day.

  • Why rotable?

    • No lead times! Components are ready when you need them to get you back online immediately. Our rotable program provides a flat rate cost for each component we offer that will help you control costs and stay on budget.
